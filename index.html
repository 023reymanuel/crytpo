Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Crypto Profit Calculator accurate?

Yes. We pull historical price data from CoinGecko and CryptoCompare APIs, which are the industry standards for accuracy. Returns are calculated based on the daily close price of the asset.

How do I calculate taxes on crypto profits?

In many jurisdictions (like the US and UK), crypto profits are subject to Capital Gains Tax. This calculator shows your gross profit. We recommend using a dedicated tax software for official reporting.

Can I calculate Ethereum or Solana ROI?

Yes. Our tool supports Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, XRP, and over 10,000 other cryptocurrencies. Use the "Select Asset" dropdown to find your coin.
