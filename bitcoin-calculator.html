Bitcoin ROI Calculator: Calculate Historical Profits

Bitcoin (BTC) has been the best-performing asset of the last decade. Many investors ask, "What if I invested $100 in Bitcoin in 2010?" The answer is life-changing. Our Bitcoin Profit Calculator allows you to calculate the exact Return on Investment (ROI) from any date in history to today.

How to use this Bitcoin Investment Calculator?

Using this tool is simple. Enter your initial investment amount (e.g., $100, $1,000) and select the date you "bought" Bitcoin. The calculator pulls historical price data from CoinGecko and compares it to the current live price of BTC. It automatically calculates:

  • Total Profit/Loss: The raw value gained.
  • Percentage Growth: The ROI percentage (often over 10,000% for early investors).
  • Current Value: What your portfolio would be worth today.

Is Bitcoin Investment taxable?

In the United States (IRS), United Kingdom (HMRC), and Canada (CRA), crypto-to-fiat profits are subject to Capital Gains Tax. While this calculator shows your gross profit, we recommend using tax software or consulting a professional to calculate your net obligations.
