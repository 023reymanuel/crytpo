Ethereum Investment FAQ

What if I invested $1,000 in Ethereum in 2016?

In 2016, Ethereum traded between $1 and $10. A $1,000 investment then would be worth millions today. Use the calculator to see the precise value based on the exact date.

Can I calculate ETH staking rewards?

This calculator shows capital gains (price increase). To calculate staking rewards (APY), you would need to add approximately 3-5% per year to the final total.
