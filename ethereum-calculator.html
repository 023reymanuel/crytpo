Ethereum Investment FAQ

Ethereum (ETH) is the backbone of DeFi and NFTs. Since its ICO in 2014, ETH has provided massive returns to early adopters. This Ethereum Investment Calculator helps you visualize the power of holding (HODLing) ETH over long periods, filtering out the daily noise of market volatility.

Ethereum vs. Bitcoin ROI

While Bitcoin is "digital gold," Ethereum often behaves like a tech stock with higher volatility. Historically, during bull markets, ETH has outperformed BTC in percentage gains. Use this tool to compare a $1,000 investment in ETH versus BTC on the same date to see which would have performed better.

Does this calculate Staking Rewards?

This tool calculates Capital Appreciation (price increase) only. It does not include Ethereum staking rewards (approx. 3-5% APY). If you staked your ETH on platforms like Binance or Lido, your actual returns would be even higher than what is shown here.
